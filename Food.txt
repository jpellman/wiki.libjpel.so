== Recipes ==

=== Oat Milk ===

 * 4 cups water
 * 1/2 tsp guar gum
 * 2 tsp Calcium Carbonate
 * 3 Tbsp date syrup or 2 Tbsp molasses or 5g of sugar

=== General Tso's Textured Vegetable Protein (TVP) ===
  
  * 1/2 cup TVP
  * 2 2/3 Tbsp sugar
  * 1 1/3 Tbsp [[https://smile.amazon.com/Sunbird-General-Chicken-Seasoning-Packet/dp/B00HVS31DC?sa-no-redirect=1|General Tso's seasoning packet]]
  * 2 Tbsp soy sauce (or perhaps worcestershire sauce)
  * 1/2 cup boiling water
  * 1 egg (optional)
